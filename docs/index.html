Welcome to GrunnSec Research Group at the University of Groningen

GrunnSec is a like minded, dynamic group of people conducting cutting-edge research on security and privacy at the University of Groningen. The group is led by prof. Fatih Turkmen and includes excellent PhD and master/bachelor students pushing the envelope on topics related to:

  • security attacks against AI systems
  • privacy-preserving, decentralized machine learning
  • fuzz testing of software and systems
  • authentication and authorization systems
